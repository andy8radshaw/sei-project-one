BATTLESHIPS

I declare WAR. Arrange your ships and lets do BATTLE!

  • Position your battleships by pressing the Randomize Player Ships button.
  • You can press this as many times as you like until you are happy with your ship's locations.
  • Now, press Start Game!
  • Take turns with the computer to drop your bombs on each others grid.
  • Once a ship has sank, move on to the next one!
  • Once all ships have sank the game is over and a winner is decided.
  • Press the reset button to start another game!
